### 1. 6-Dimensional Wellness Scoring ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**What it does:**
- Real-time calculation of 6 wellness metrics
- Sophisticated algorithms analyzing 100+ data points
- ML-inspired trend analysis
- Personalized wellness index

**The 6 Dimensions:**
1. **Posture Score** (0-100)
   - Forward head detection
   - Slouching analysis
   - Spinal alignment tracking
   - Real-time feedback

2. **Eye Health Score** (0-100)
   - Blink rate optimization (15-20/min target)
   - Eye fatigue detection
   - Eye closure duration tracking
   - Strain assessment

3. **Focus Score** (0-100)
   - Screen attention percentage
   - Head movement analysis
   - Gaze direction tracking
   - Attention stability metrics

4. **Fatigue Score** (0-100, inverse)
   - Yawn frequency detection
   - Eye closure patterns
   - Blink rate changes
   - Session duration impact

5. **Productivity Score** (0-100)
   - Composite of posture, focus, eye health
   - Fatigue penalties
- Performance optimization tracking

6. **Wellness Index** (0-100)
   - Overall health indicator
   - Weighted average of all metrics
   - Holistic well-being assessment
   - Session quality measurement

**Technical Features:**
- Updates every 2 seconds
- Smooth animations
- Trend tracking
- Historical comparison
- Insight generation
